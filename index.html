Company Profile

With 30-plus years’ experience in making hot press forming equipment, we are a leader in the field, handling diversified vacuum-type rubber forming machines, rubber injection molding machines, thin-film forming lines, hot press testers, air-assisted high-pressure forming lines and many other customized machinery. Backed by seasoned and creative R&D team, we are willing to develop all kinds of to-order servo/energy-efficient forming lines. Inquiries and overseas agents welcome!
